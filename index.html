Built from one heartfelt surprise into a growing celebration brand

Surprisewala began with a simple personal mission, making loved ones happy through thoughtful, emotionally charged surprises. What started as a birthday gesture grew into a brand focused on creating joy through cakes, decor, gifts, and unforgettable reveal moments.

The spirit of the brand is clear: spread happiness, create memories that last, and make every customer feel like their special moment has been treated with real care.

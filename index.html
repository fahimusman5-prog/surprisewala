Our Little Story

From one birthday surprise to a little happiness business.

What started as a birthday surprise became a love language for hundreds of families, couples and friends.

I was always passionate about making others happy through cute little surprises. It all started in 2019, when I planned birthday surprises for my boyfriend.

During 2020, everything slowed down because of Covid-19. But in 2021, even with restrictions, I managed to arrange a theatre surprise for his birthday. The appreciation I received from people around me and the theatre management motivated me to start this as a business.

On 1st September 2021, I opened the Surprisewala Instagram page. At first, I could not focus much because of office work, so I only arranged a few surprises for people close to me.

The real turning point came on 24th March 2023, when I planned my first ever midnight car surprise for my boyfriend's birthday. From that day, there was no looking back. Surprise events started growing, and with the support of my parents and loved ones, Surprisewala became something bigger.

I am truly grateful to Almighty, to my boyfriend who has been my biggest supporter and strength, to everyone who supported me, and to all our customers who believed in Surprisewala to surprise their loved ones.
